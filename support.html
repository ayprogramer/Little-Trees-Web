FAQ

How do I reset my streak?
There is no streak reset option. You can reset all app data from the Settings page.
How do I add friends?
Sign in, then go to the Friends tab and enter your friend's unique code to connect.
What happens if I go over my app limit?
Your tree resets, and you can start fresh the next day.
