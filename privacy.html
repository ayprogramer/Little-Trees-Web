Privacy Policy for Little Trees: Screen Time

Effective Date: October 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Little Trees: Screen Time handles your information.

Information We Collect

  • App usage data: We store your streaks, blocked app settings, and progress locally on your device.
  • Friends feature: If you choose to add friends, the app shares streak information only with people you connect with.
  • We do not collect personal data such as your name, address, or payment details.

How We Use Information

  • To provide the app's core features (screen time tracking, streaks, and tree growth).
  • To allow you to connect with friends for motivation and accountability.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Some data may be synced if you connect with friends, but we do not sell or share your information with third parties.
